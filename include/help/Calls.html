Overview

ProTracker enables you to create a record of a call along with information regarding the subject of discussion, the related client or contact, the participants, the time period, and the status. You can invite participants to the call when you create the record or at a later date. You can create a note or add attachments before or after a call occurs. After you complete a call, or if you cancel it for any reason, you can update its status.

Availability

The Accounts module is available to the following editions:

  • Standard
  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Log or create a call

Use the Create Calls form to log (or create) a call record.
  1. Access the form using one of the following:
    • In the Navigation menu click Calls > Log Call
    • Click Create New in the Calls List View or Calls Detail View.
      The Calls Create form displays.
  2. Enter information in the fields of the Overview dashlet as described below. Fields that have an asterisk (*) are required; you must enter data in the field to save the call.

    Related to:
    1. Click the related record type from this dropdown.
    2. Click the item on the related record type Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of items matching the character(s) you enter. That is, for a call about a client, you would click Client, and then click the client name on the Client Popup List View.

    Subject:*

    Enter the subject of discussion.

    Start Date & Time:*

    In the Date Selector click the date of the call. Then click the time, in hours and minutes and the period of the day, from the adjoining dropdowns.

    End Date:

    Click the date in the calendar pop-up and click the time, in hours and minutes and the period of the day, from the adjoining dropdown.

    Duration:*

    Enter the number of hours and click the number of minutes from the drop-down list.

    Description:

    Enter a brief description of the purpose of the call.

    Assigned to:

    By default, the call is assigned to the creator of the Calls record. To change the assignment, click the user on the Users Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of users matching the character(s) you enter.

    Activity Type:

    Click the activity type on the Activities Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of activities matching the character(s) you enter.

    Status:*

    Choose a status based on the following:

    If…

    Click…

    It is an inbound call

    Inbound

    It is an outbound call

    Outbound

    The call is scheduled to take place

    Planned

    The call is completed

    Held

    The call did not occur

    Not Held

    Email Reminder Time:

    Click the time prior to the call to send an email reminder.

    Reminders: Popup and Email all invitees

    Click a reminder type, then click the time prior to the call to send the reminder to call invitees.

    ProTracker will send a reminder to all participants at the specified time. If you set a default time for reminders on your User Profile, ProTracker will implement it when you do not set a reminder during call creation.

    Attachment:
    1. Click Choose File.
    2. Navigate to and click the file you want to attach.
    3. Click Open.
      The filename displays.

    Teams

    By default, your private team is assigned as the primary team. To change the primary team assignment, click the team on the Teams Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of teams matching the character(s) you enter.

    To assign additional teams to the Calls record, click add (+) and click the team as described above. For more information on assigning teams, see Assigning records to Users and Teams.

    Description

    Enter a brief description of the purpose of the call.

    Add Invitees
    1. In the Add Invitees dashlet, enter all or part of an invitee’s first name, last name or email address in the First Name:, Last Name:, or Email:  fields, respectively.
    2. Click Search.
      A list of records matching the search criteria displays.
    3. Click Add  to add each person you want to invite.
      The invitee’s name is added to the Scheduling pane.
  3. Choose one of the following options.

    Save.

    Save the call and send invitations to the participants

    Save & Send.
    The Calls Detail View displays, and the invitations are sent.

    Create a copy of the call, putting the original call on hold, while retaining all of the information of the original record (except for the status).

    Close and Create New
    The original call closes with a status of Held. The new call displays in the Calls Create form.

View and process calls

Click Calls > View Calls on the Navigation menu to display the list of calls. Use the choices below to process your calls.

To….

Then…

Sort the list of calls

Click any column title; to reverse the sort order, click the column title again.

Find a specific call
  1. Click Basic Search to search by the Start Date, Subject, and/or Related to fields; click Advanced Search to search more fields.
    The Basic Search form or Advanced Search form displays.
  2. Click to select one or more values in one or more fields.
  3. Click Search.
    The Calls List View updates, displaying only calls that satisfy your search criteria.

To see the whole list of calls again, click Clear, then click Search.

View the details of a call

Click the Subject of the call in the Calls List View.
The details and associated sub-panels display.

Edit a call
  1. Click the associated edit (pencil) icon. Alternatively, you can click Edit on the Calls Detail View.
    The detail view displays in edit mode.
  2. Revise the information
  3. Click Save.

Suppress one or more calls
  1. Click the associated selection box for each call you want to update.
  2. Click Suppress.
    A confirmation popup displays.
  3. Click OK.
    The call is suppressed and the updated Calls List View displays.

For more information, see Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Update the information for one more calls
  1. Click the associated selection box for each call you want to update.
  2. Click Mass Update in the Action dropdown.

For more information, see Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Export information for one or more calls
  1. Click the associated selection box for each call you want to export.
  2. Click Export in the Action dropdown.

For more information, see Exporting data.

From the Calls Detail View, you can do the following.

To….

Then…

Edit the call
  1. Click Edit.
    The detail view displays in edit mode.
  2. Revise the information
  3. Click Save.

Duplicate a call, conveniently creating a similar call

Click Duplicate in the dropdown.
The original call closes, and the duplicate Calls Edit View displays.

You can change the information in the duplicate call and save it.

Suppress the call
  1. Click Suppress in the dropdown.

    The confirmation popup displays.

  2. Click OK.

    The call is suppressed and the Calls List View displays.

Close and create a new call
  1. Click Close and Create New in the dropdown.
    The call closes and the Calls Create form displays.
  2. Complete the form and save the call.

Close the Calls Detail View and return to the Calls List View

Click Close in the dropdown.

Edit a related record
  1. Click the record in the sub-panel.
    The associated view of the record displays.
  2. Edit the record and save it. See the documentation for the type of record for more information.

Remove an association to another record
  1. Click Remove.
  2. Click OK in the confirmation popup.
