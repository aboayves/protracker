Overview

Use the Goals module to track a client's use of Goals.

Availability

The Goals module is available to Administrators of the following editions:

  • Standard
  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

Create a Goal record

Use the following instructions to create a Goal record.

  1. Access the Goals Create form by doing one of the following:
    • Click Goals & Objectives > Goals > Create Goals on the Navigation menu.
    • Click Clients > View Clients on the Navigation menu, click the name of the client, and on the Client View Detail Goals sub-panel Actions dropdown click Create.
  2. Complete the form by entering information for the fields described below. Fields that have an asterisk (*) are required; you must enter data in the field to save the goal.
  3. Click Save.
    The Goal record displays in the Goals List View.

Client*

Click the Client associated with this goal on the Clients Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or on the list of Clients matching the character(s) you enter.

Assigned to

By default, the record is assigned to the creator of the Goals record. To change the assignment, click the user on the Users Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of users matching the character(s) you enter.

Goal*

Describe the client's goal. This is a mandatory field.

Target date

Click on the Date Selector the date the Goal should be reached.

Amount

Enter a dollar value representing the total amount of money needed to reach the goal; e.g., a mortgage down payment.

Goal Type

Click the broad category that includes the client's goal.

Progress

Enter the progress towards the goal as a percentage.

Description

Enter additional details about the Goal.

Teams*

By default, your private team is assigned as the primary team. To change the primary team assignment, click the team on the Teams Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of teams matching the character(s) you enter. This is a mandatory field.

To assign additional teams to the Goal record, click add (+) and click the team as described above. For more information on assigning teams, see Assigning Users and Teams to Records.

Manage Goal records

Use the following instructions to perform tasks such as exporting or suppressing Goal records.

To…

Then…

Display list of a Goal records

Click Goals & Objectives > Goals > View Goals.

Sort the Goal records in the Goals List View

Click on any column title; to reverse the sort order, click the column title again.

Display the details of a Goal record

Click the name of the Goal in the Goals List View.

Edit the Goal record

  1. Click the edit (pencil) icon associated with the Goal you want to update in the Goals List View.
    The Goals Edit View displays.
  2. Update the information using the field descriptions in Create a Goal.
  3. Click Save.
    The Goal record is updated.

Edit many Goal records at once

  1. Click the selection boxes associated with the Goal records you want to update in the Goals List View.
  2. Click Mass Update on the Actions dropdown.
  3. Follow the instructions in Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Suppress multiple Goal records

  1. In the Goals List View, click the selection box associated with the records you want to suppress.
  2. Click Suppress.
    A confirmation box displays.
  3. Click OK.
    The records are suppressed.

For more information, see  Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Alternatively, to suppress a single record, you can click Suppress on the Goals Detail View.

Duplicate a Goal record

Click Duplicate on the Goals Detail View Actions dropdown. Duplication is a convenient way of creating a similar Goal record. You can change the information in the duplicate record to create a new Goal.

Merge duplicate records
  1. In the Goals List View click the selection box associated with the records you want to merge.
  2. Click Merge in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Merging duplicate records.

Export records
  1. In the Goals List View, click the selection box associated with the records you want to export.
  2. Click Export in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Exporting data.
