Overview

Use the Contacts module to create and manage contacts for your organization.

A contact is any individual who is a valid sales lead; a lead that has been evaluated and assessed for a possible sales opportunity. You can associate Contacts with any record such as a Client, Opportunity, or Campaign. Associating Contacts with a Campaign enables you to track the effectiveness of the campaign in generating opportunities for your organization.

Availability

The Contacts module is available to the following editions:

  • Standard
  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

The Navigation menu displays the following Contacts actions:

  • Create Contact
  • Create Contact From vCard
  • View Contacts
  • View Contact Reports
  • Import Contacts

Manage Contacts

Follow the instructions below to perform tasks such as editing, importing and exporting contact records.

To…

Then…

Suppress multiple contacts
  1. In the Contacts list, click the selection box associated with the records you want to suppress.
  2. Click Suppress.
    A confirmation box displays.
  3. Click OK.

    The contacts are suppressed.

Update information related to multiple contacts
  1. In the Contacts list, click the selection box associated with the records you want to update.
  2. Click Mass Update in the dropdown.

For more information, see   Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Add one or more contacts to a campaign target list
  1. In the Contacts list, click the selection box associated with the records you want to add.
  2. Click Add to Target List in the dropdown.
    The Target Lists displays.
  3. Click the list.
    The contact is added to the campaign target list.

Display the detail view for a contact

Click the contact name in the Contact List.
The detail displays.

Edit contact information

Click Edit in the contact’s detail view.

Import contact records

Click Import Contacts from the Navigation menu and follow the instructions in    Importing data.

Export records
  1. In the Contacts list, click the selection box associated with the records you want to export.
  2. Click Export in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Exporting data.

Merge duplicate records
  1. In the Contacts list, click the selection box associated with the records you want to merge.
  2. Click Merge in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Merging duplicate records.

Duplicate contact information

Click Duplicate in the Contacts Detail View. Duplication is a convenient way of creating a similar contact. You can change the information in the duplicate record to create a new contact.

Archive an email associated with a contact

In the Contact Detail view History sub-panel, click Archive Email.

Using vCards to Import and Export Contacts

vCards are electronic business cards that are exchanged through applications such as email and web browsers. You can view and create a vCard file from the record’s Detail View. You can save it to your local machine to import it to other applications.

When you create contacts in ProTracker, or import them from another application, the system automatically creates a vCard for it. You can also create a vCard file from the record’s Detail View and save it in Outlook, or on your local machine.

To…

Then…

To import a vCard
  1. Click Create Contact from vCard from the Navigation menu.
    The Import vCard page displays.
  2. Click Browse to navigate to the vCard location on the local file system.
  3. Click on the file from your local machine
  4. Click Import vCard.
    The Contacts Detail View of the created contact displays.

To edit or to save a vCard to a different location
  1. Click the vCard icon located next to the contact’s name in the Contacts Detail View.
    This displays a dialog box prompting you to edit or save the file.
  2. To edit the vCard and save it in your Contacts folder in Outlook, open the file, make the necessary changes and click Save; to save the vCard on your local machine, select Save As.
