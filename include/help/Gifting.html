Overview

Use the Gifting module to track a client's use of Giftings.

Availability

The Gifting module is available to Administrators of the following editions:

  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

Create a Gift record

Use the following instructions to create a Gift record.

  1. Access the Giftings Create form by doing one of the following:
    • Click Estate Planning > Gifting > Create Gift on the Navigation menu.
    • Click Clients > View Clients on the Navigation menu, click the name of the client, and on the Client View Detail Giftings sub-panel Actions dropdown click Create.
  2. Complete the form by entering information for the fields described below. Fields that have an asterisk (*) are required; you must enter data in the field to save the Gifting.
  3. Click Save.
    The Gift record displays in the Giftings List View.

Gift*

Describe the Gift. This is a mandatory field.

Gift Recipient

Enter the name of the person who received the gift.

Contact

Click the Contact on the Contacts Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or on the list of Contacts matching the character(s) you enter.

Client

Click the Client on the Clients Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or on the list of Clients matching the character(s) you enter.

Date of Gift*

Click on the Date Selector the date the gift was given. This is a mandatory field.

Gift Value*

Enter the cash value of the gift. This is a mandatory field.

709 Required

Click the checkbox if a federal gift tax form is required (Form 709).

Date 709 Filed

Click on the Date Selector the date the Form 709 was filed.

Date Crummey Notice Sent

Click on the Date Selector the date a Crummey Notice was sent to the beneficiaries of a trust informing them of a gift to the trust and their withdrawal rights.

Description

Enter additional details about the gift.

Manage Gift records

Use the following instructions to perform tasks such as exporting or suppressing Gift records.

To…

Then…

Display list of a Gift records

Click Estate Planning > Gifting > View Gifts.

Sort the Gift records in the Giftings List View

Click on any column title; to reverse the sort order, click the column title again.

Display the details of a Gift record

Click the name of the Gift in the Giftings List View.

Edit the Gift record

  1. Click the edit (pencil) icon associated with the Gift you want to update in the Gifting List View.
    The Giftings Edit View displays.
  2. Update the information using the field descriptions in Create a Gift.
  3. Click Save.
    The Gift record is updated.

Edit many Gift records at once

  1. Click the selection boxes associated with the Gift records you want to update in the Gifting List View.
  2. Click Mass Update on the Actions dropdown.
  3. Follow the instructions in Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Suppress multiple Gift records

  1. In the Giftings List View, click the selection box associated with the records you want to suppress.
  2. Click Suppress.
    A confirmation box displays.
  3. Click OK.
    The records are suppressed.

For more information, see  Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Alternatively, to suppress a single record, you can click Suppress on the Giftings Detail View.

Merge duplicate records
  1. In the Giftings List View click the selection box associated with the records you want to merge.
  2. Click Merge in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Merging duplicate records.

Export records
  1. In the Giftings List View, click the selection box associated with the records you want to export.
  2. Click Export in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Exporting data.
