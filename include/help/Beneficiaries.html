Overview

Use the Beneficiaries module to document the beneficiaries of an Account.

Availability

The Beneficiaries module is available to users of the following editions:

  • Standard
  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

Create a Beneficiary record

  1. Access the Beneficiaries Create form by doing one of the following:
    • Click Accounts > Beneficiaries > Create Beneficiaries on the Navigation menu.
    • Click Accounts > Accounts > View Accounts from the Navigation menu, click the related account to view its Beneficiaries sub-panel, and then click Create on the Beneficiaries Actions dropdown.
  2. Complete the form by entering information for the fields described below. Fields that have an asterisk (*) are required; you must enter data in the field to save the Beneficiary record.
  3. Click Save.
    The Beneficiaries record is added.

Account

Click the Account to which this person is a beneficiary on the Accounts Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of Accounts matching the character(s) you enter.

Beneficiary

Click the beneficiary name on the Contacts Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of Contacts matching the character(s) you enter.

Beneficiary Type

Click the type of Beneficiary such as primary or secondary.

Percent

Enter the percentage of the account the Beneficiary will receive.

Description

Enter additional details about the Beneficiary Record.

Assigned to

By default, the record is assigned to the creator of the Beneficiaries record. To change the assignment, click the user on the Users Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of users matching the character(s) you enter.

Beneficiary Name

If the beneficiary does not exist in the contact list and you do not intend to add a Contact record for the beneficiary, enter the name of the beneficiary.

Date Verified

In the Date Selector click the date the beneficiary information was verified.

Per Stirpes

Click this check box if the account is to be distributed per stirpes. Per stirpes means that an asset is to be distributed in equal shares to each heir. If an heir predeceases the decedent, then the heir's portion will be distributed in equal shares to his or her children.

Teams*

By default, your private team is assigned as the primary team. To change the primary team assignment, click the team on the Teams Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of teams matching the character(s) you enter.

To assign additional teams to the Beneficiaries record, click add (+) and click the team as described above. For more information on assigning teams, see Assigning Users and Teams to Records.

This is a mandatory field; the Beneficiary record must be assigned to a Team.

Edit a Beneficiary Record

  1. Access the Beneficiaries Edit View by doing one of the following:
    • Click Accounts > Beneficiaries > View Beneficiaries on the Navigation menu, then click the edit (pencil) icon on the Beneficiary record you want to edit.
    • Click Accounts > Accounts > View Accounts from the Navigation menu, click the related account to view its Beneficiaries sub-panel, and then click Edit on the Beneficiaries Actions dropdown.
  2. Complete the form using the field descriptions from Create a Beneficiary record.
  3. Click Save.
    The changes to the Beneficiaries record are saved.

Suppress a Beneficiary Record

  1. Click Accounts > Beneficiaries > View Beneficiaries on the Navigation menu.
    The Beneficiaries List View displays.
  2. Click the Selection box for each record you want to suppress.
  3. Click Suppress on the Beneficiaries Actions dropdown.
    The record(s) are suppressed.
