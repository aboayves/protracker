Overview

Use the Account Owners module to document account ownership. All users who can view and edit accounts can view and edit Account Owner records. The Account Owners module is independent of the Accounts ownership type field which is used to control the organization of accounts in reports.

Availability

The Account Owners module is available to users of the following editions:

  • Standard
  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

Create an Account Owner

  1. Access the Account Owners Create form by doing one of the following:
    • Click Accounts > Account Owners > Create Account Owners on the Navigation menu.
    • Click Accounts > Accounts from the Navigation menu, click the related account to view its Account Owners sub-panel, and then click Create on the Account Owners Actions dropdown.
  2. Complete the form by entering information for the following fields:

    Account*

    Click the account to which the account owner applies on the Account Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of Accounts matching the character(s) you enter. This is a mandatory field; you must click an Account.

    Contact

    Click the Contact who owns a portion of this account from the Contact Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of Contacts matching the character(s) you enter. This is a mandatory field; you must click a Contact.

    Ownership Percent*

    Enter the portion of the account owned by the Contact; e.g., enter 33 if the Contact owns 33% of the Account. This is a mandatory field; you must enter a percentage.

    Description

    Enter additional details about the account ownership.
  3. Click Save.
    The Account Owner record is added.

Edit an Account Owner

  1. Access the Account Owner Edit View by doing one of the following:
    • Click Accounts > Account Owners > View Account Owners from the Navigation menu and click the edit (pencil) icon on the record you want to edit.
    • Click Accounts > Accounts from the Navigation menu, click the related account to view its Account Owners sub-panel, and click Edit on the Actions dropdown.
  2. Complete the form by entering information for the form fields as described in Create an Account Owner.
  3. Click Save.
    The updates are saved to the Account Owner record.

Suppress an Account Owner

  1. Click Accounts > Account Owners > View Account Owners from the Navigation menu.
    The Account Owners List View displays.
  2. Click the selection box associated with the record(s) you want to suppress.
  3. Click Suppress in the Actions dropdown.
    The confirmation box displays.
  4. Click OK.
    The records are suppressed.
