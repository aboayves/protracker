Overview

Use the Gift Tax Credits module to track a client's use of gift tax credits.

Availability

The Gift Tax Credits module is available to Administrators of the following editions:

  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

Create a Gift Tax Credit

Use the following instructions to create a Gift Tax Credit.

  1. Access the Gift Tax Credits Create form by doing one of the following:
    • Click Estate Planning > Gift Tax Credits > Create Gift Tax Credit on the Navigation menu.
    • Click Clients > View Clients on the Navigation menu, click the name of the client, and on the Client View Detail Gift Tax Credits sub-panel Actions dropdown click Create.
  2. Complete the form by entering information for the fields described below. Fields that have an asterisk (*) are required; you must enter data in the field to save the Gift Tax Credit.
  3. Click Save.
    The Gift Tax Credit displays in the Gift Tax Credits List View.

Gift Tax Credit*

Describe the Gift Tax Credit transaction. This is a mandatory field.

Contact

Enter the contact who used the gift tax credit.

Client

Click the Client on the Clients Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or on the list of Clients matching the character(s) you enter.

Credit Amount*

Enter the cash value of the gift tax credit. This is a mandatory field.

Date Credit Used*

Click on the Date Selector the date of the gift tax credit transaction. This is a mandatory field.

Description

Enter additional details about the gift tax credit transaction.

Manage Gift Tax Credits

Use the following instructions to perform tasks such as exporting or suppressing Gift Tax Credit records.

To…

Then…

Display list of a Gift Tax Credit records

Click Estate Planning > Gift Tax Credits > View Gift Tax Credits.

Sort the Gift Tax Credits records in the Gift Tax Credits List View

Click on any column title; to reverse the sort order, click the column title again.

Display the details of a Gift Tax Credit record

Click the name of the Gift Tax Credit in the Gift Tax Credits List View.

Edit the Gift Tax Credit record

  1. Click the edit (pencil) icon associated with the Gift Tax Credit you want to update in the Gift Tax Credit List View.
    The Gift Tax Credits Edit View displays.
  2. Update the information using the field descriptions in Create a Gift Tax Credit.
  3. Click Save.
    The Gift Tax Credit record is updated.

Suppress multiple Gift Tax Credit records

  1. In the Gift Tax Credits List View, click the selection box associated with the records you want to suppress.
  2. Click Suppress.
    A confirmation box displays.
  3. Click OK.
    The records are suppressed.

For more information, see  Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Alternatively, to suppress a single record, you can click Suppress on the Gift Tax Credits Detail View.

Merge duplicate records
  1. In the Gift Tax Credits List View click the selection box associated with the records you want to merge.
  2. Click Merge in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Merging duplicate records.

Export records
  1. In the Gift Tax Credits List View, click the selection box associated with the records you want to export.
  2. Click Export in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Exporting data.
