Overview

Use the Companies module to create and manage businesses with which your organization does not directly interact, e.g.; a financial institution, employer, or insurance company. If a company is a client of your organization or you need to keep detailed records of activities, use the Contacts module instead.

Availability

The Clients module is available to the following editions:

  • Standard
  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

The Navigation menu displays the following Companies actions (an item of the Relationships menu):

  • Create Companies
  • View Companies
  • Import Companies

Create a Company

Use the following steps to create a Company:

  1. In the Navigation men, click Relationships -> Companies -> Create Companies.
    The Create Companies form displays in edit mode.
  2. Enter the following information in the Company Profile dashlet. Fields that have an asterisk (*) are required; you must enter data in the field to save the Company.

    Name*

    Enter the Company’s legal name

    Short Name

    Enter an abbreviation or short name to describe the company

    Industry

    From this dropdown, click the industrial sector to which the company belongs.

    Employees

    Enter the number of employees in the company

    Master Account

    Enter the Master Account Number assigned by the financial institution to aggregate client accounts. Master account numbers are assigned by both Fidelity and Charles Schwab. It is a way for these custodians to group all advisor accounts together. When a custodian sends a file it may contain multiple accounts. Some advisers have both discretionary and non-discretionary accounts. The custodian will assign separate Master account numbers for each. That is to say, there will be a master account number for all discretionary accounts, and another master account for non-discretionary accounts. If multiple master account numbers are used, create a company record for each.

    Description

    Enter a description of the company.

    Ticker Symbol

    Enter the stock ticker symbol for the company.

    Ownership

    Specify the company’s owner information.

    Annual Revenue

    Enter the organization’s annual revenue.

    Routing Number

    Enter the routing number.

    Teams*

    By default, your private team is assigned as the primary team. To change the primary team assignment, click the team on the Teams List (displays upon clicking the selection arrow) or the list of teams matching the character(s) you enter in the text entry box. To assign additional teams to the client, click add (+) and click the team. For more information on assigning teams, see Assigning records to Users and Teams.
  3. Enter the following information in the Address & Phone dashlet.

    Office Phone

    Enter the Company’s primary office phone number.

    Alternate Phone

    Enter the Company’s alternate phone number such as an 800 number.

    Email

    Enter the Company’s email addresses. To add an additional email address, click the Add icon. If the client has more than one email address, click the radial next to the primary email address. If you sent out campaign emails this client chose to opt-out of receiving them, click Opted Out.

    Click Invalid to indicate an incorrect email address.

    Shipping Address dashlet

    Street:

    Enter the client’s shipping street address.

    Shipping City:

    Enter the city for the shipping address.

    State:

    Enter the state for the shipping address.

    Postal Code:

    Enter the postal code for the shipping address.

    Country:

    Enter the country for the shipping address.

    Copy address from left:

    Click the checkbox to copy the Billing Address information into this Shipping Address dashlet.

    Fax

    Enter the Company’s fax number.

    Email

    Enter the Company’s email addresses.

    Website

    Enter the Company’s website address.

    Billing Address dashlet

    Street:

    Enter the client’s billing street address.

    Billing City:

    Enter the city for the billing address.

    State:

    Enter the state for the billing address.

    Postal Code:

    Enter the postal code for the billing address.

    Country:

    Enter the country for the billing address.

    Copy address from left:

    Click the checkbox to copy the Mailing Address into this Alternate Address section.
  4. Click Save  to save the record and view its detail page. Or click Cancel  to return to the Companies List View.

Manage Companies

Managing Companies includes tasks such as editing, importing and exporting records. To manage Companies, use the following instructions.

To…

Then…

Sort the Companies List View

Click any column title that has the sort icon beside it. To reverse the sort order, click the column title again.

Display the Companies Detail View

Click on the company’s name in the Companies List View.

Edit a company’s information

Click Edit  in the Companies Detail View.

Suppress multiple companies
  1. In the Companies list, click the selection box associated with the records you want to suppress.
  2. Click Suppress.
    A confirmation box displays.
  3. Click OK.
    The companies are suppressed.

For more information, see  Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Alternatively, you can click Suppress on the Companies Detail View.

Update information related to multiple companies
  1. In the Clients list, click the selection box associated with the records you want to update.
  2. Click Mass Update in the dropdown.

For more information, see  Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Import company records

Click Relationships -> Companies -> Import from the Navigation menu and follow the instructions in  Importing data.

Export records
  1. In the Companies List View, click the selection box associated with the records you want to export.
  2. Click Export in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Exporting data.

Merge duplicate records
  1. In the Companies List View, click the selection box associated with the records you want to merge.
  2. Click Merge in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Merging duplicate records.

Duplicate Company information

Click Duplicate in the Companies Detail View. Duplication is a convenient way of creating a similar company record. You can change the information in the duplicate record to create a new company record.
