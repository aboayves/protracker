Overview

Use the Income in Respect of a Decedent module to record income a decedent had a right to receive that is not includable in the final tax return. For a cash basis decedent, IRD is income earned bit not yet received prior to death. IRD is taxable income to the recipient the year received. If IRD is paid to the estate, it is reported on the fiduciary return. If IRD is paid directly to a beneficiary, it is reported on the beneficiary's tax return.

Availability

The IRD module is available to users of the following editions:

  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

Create an IRD record

  1. Access the IRD Create form by doing one of the following:
    • Click Estate Planning > IRD > Create IRD on the Navigation menu.
    • Click Clients > View Clients from the Navigation menu, click the related account to view its IRD sub-panel, and then click Create on the IRD Actions dropdown.
  2. Complete the form by entering information for the fields described below. Fields that have an asterisk (*) are required; you must enter data in the field to save the IRD record.
  3. Click Save.
    The IRD record is added.

Income*

Describe the income received by the decendent. This is a mandatory field.

Decedent

Click the deceased contact who received the income on the Contacts Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of Contact matching the character(s) you enter.

Client

Click the Client to which the IRD record applies on the Clients Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of Clients matching the character(s) you enter.

Income Date*

Click on the Date Selector the date the income was received. This is a mandatory field.

Amount*

Enter the income amount. This is a mandatory field.

Description

Enter additional details about the IRD.

Edit IRD records

Follow these instructions to edit an IRD record.

  1. Access the IRD Edit View by doing one of the following:
    • Click Compliance > IRD > View IRD on the Navigation menu, and click the edit (pencil) icon for the record you want to edit.
    • Click Clients > View Clients from the Navigation menu, click the related account to view its IRD sub-panel, and then click Edit on the IRD Actions dropdown.
  2. Make changes using the field descriptions in Create an IRD record.
  3. Click Save.
    The IRD record is updated.

To edit many records at once, follow these instructions:

  1. Click the selection boxes associated with the records you want to update in the IRD List View.
  2. Click Mass Update on the Actions dropdown.
  3. Follow the instructions in Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Suppress IRD records

To suppress an IRD record, use the following instructions.
  1. Click Compliance > Income in Respect of a Decedent > View Income in Respect of a Decedent on the Navigation menu.
    The Income in Respect of a Decedent List View displays.
  2. Click the selection box for each record you want to suppress.
  3. Click Suppress.
    The records are removed.

For more information see Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Merge duplicate IRD records

To merge duplicate IRD records, use the following instructions.
  1. Click Compliance > Income in Respect of a Decedent > View Income in Respect of a Decedent on the Navigation menu.
    The Income in Respect of a Decedent List View displays.
  2. Click the selection box for each record you want to merge.
  3. Click Merge in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Merging duplicate records.

Export IRD records

To suppress an IRD record, use the following instructions.
  1. Click Compliance > IRD > View IRD on the Navigation menu.
    The IRD List View displays.
  2. Click the selection box for each record you want to export.
  3. Click Export in the dropdown and follow the instructions in Exporting data.

For more information see Editing and suppressing multiple records.
