Overview

Use the Life Insurance module to track a client's Life insurance policies.

Availability

The Life Insurance module is available to users of the following editions:

  • Standard
  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

Create a Life Insurance record

  1. Access the Life Insurance Create form by doing one of the following:
    • Click Insurance > Life Insurance > Create Life Insurance on the Navigation menu.
    • Click Clients > View Clients from the Navigation menu, click the related account to view its Life Insurance sub-panel, and then click Create on the Life Insurance Actions dropdown.
  2. Complete the form by entering information for the fields described below. Fields that have an asterisk (*) are required; you must enter data in the field to save the Life Insurance record.
  3. Click Save.
    The Life Insurance record is added.

Client

Click the Client to which the Life Insurance record applies on the Clients Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of Clients matching the character(s) you enter.

Insured Contact

Click the Contact who is insured by this policy on the Contacts Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of Contacts matching the character(s) you enter.

Company

Click the Company that issued the insurance policy on the Companies Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of Companies matching the character(s) you enter.

Policy Number

Enter the policy number issued by the insurance company.

Policy Type

Click the type of policy.

File

Click Choose File and navigate to and select the file containing a copy of the insurance policy. The file name displays in the Select File box.

Policy Status

Click the current status of this policy.

Issue Date

Click on the Date Selector the date the policy was originally issued.

Effective Date

Click on the Date Selector the date the policy became effective.

End Date

Click on the Date Selector the date the policy ends.

Endorsements

Describe any special endorsements contained in the policy. If you prefer not to enter specific details about this insurance policy in the designated fields, use the endorsements field for a general description of the policy coverage. For example, if you track policy details in a separate system, copy and paste the policy info here.

Assigned to

By default, the record is assigned to the creator of the Life Insurance record. To change the assignment, click the user on the Users Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of users matching the character(s) you enter.

Description

Enter additional information about the policy.

Teams*

By default, your private team is assigned as the primary team. To change the primary team assignment, click the team on the Teams Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of teams matching the character(s) you enter.

To assign additional teams to the Life Insurance record click add (+) and click the team as described above. For more information on assigning teams, see Assigning Users and Teams to Records.

This is a mandatory field; you must assign a Team.

Edit a Life Insurance record

Follow these instructions to edit a Life Insurance record.

  1. Access the Life Insurance Edit View by doing one of the following:
    • Click Insurance > Life Insurance > View Life Insurance on the Navigation menu, and click the edit (pencil) icon for the record you want to edit.
    • Click Clients > View Clients from the Navigation menu, click the related account to view its Life Insurance sub-panel, and then click Edit on the Life Insurance Actions dropdown.
  2. Make changes using the field descriptions in Create a Life Insurance record.
  3. Click Save.
    The Life Insurance record is updated.

Suppress a Life Insurance record

To suppress a Life Insurance record, use the following instructions.
  1. Click Insurance > Life Insurance > View Life Insurance on the Navigation menu.
    The Life Insurance List View displays.
  2. Click the Selection box for each record you want to suppress.
  3. Click Suppress.
    The records are removed.
