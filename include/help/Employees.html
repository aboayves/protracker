Overview

Use Employees to view information such as name, title, and email address for employees in your organization.

Note the following:

  • All ProTracker users are employees, not all employees are ProTracker users.
  • Users can only view Employee records. Administrators can create and manage Employee records.
  • An employee needs a username and password to log into ProTracker to be a user.

Availability

The Employees module is available to users of the following editions:

  • Standard
  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

The Profile menu displays the Employees item.

Create an Employee record

As an administrator of ProTracker, you can create and manage Employees. When you create an employee record, it is automatically added to the Employees list as well as the Users list. Similarly, when you delete an employee, the associated user record is also deleted.

  1. Click Employees from the Profile menu (located in the upper right corner of the ProTracker window).
    The Employees Search List displays.
  2. Click Create.
    The Employees Create form displays.
  3. Enter the following information in the form. Fields that have an asterisk (*) are required; you must enter data in the field to save the Employee.

    First Name

    Enter the employee’s first name.

    Last Name*

    Enter the employee’s last name.

    Position

    Enter the employee’s position within the firm

    Description

    Enter any comments concerning the employee.

    Picture

    If you have downloaded the person’s picture in .jpg or .png format on your computer, click Browse to navigate to the location and upload the picture into the record.

    Employee Status

    From the dropdown, click one of the following status options:

    If…

    Click…

    The person is a current employee of your organization

    Active

    The employee is no longer working for your organization

    Terminated

    The employee is going to be absent from work for an extended period of time

    Leave of Absence

    Reports to

    Enter the name of the employee’s supervisor.

    Default Team

    Enter the name of the team.

    In the Address & Phone section

    Work Phone

    Enter the employee’s office phone number.

    Mobile Phone

    Enter the employee’s cell phone number.

    Home Phone

    Enter the home phone number for the employee.

    Primary Address

    Enter the employee’s primary address.

    Fax

    Enter the fax number for the employee.

    IM Type

    From the dropdown click the type of Instant Messenger service that the employee has.

    IM Name

    Enter the employee’s user name for the IM service.

    Email Address

    Enter the employee’s email addresses. To add an additional email address, click add (+). If the employee has more than one email address, click the radial next to the primary email address.

    In the Permissions section

    Is ProTracker User

    Click the checkbox if the employee is a ProTracker user.

    Is Administrator

    Click the checkbox if the employee is a ProTracker Administrator

    Portal API User

    Click the checkbox if the employee is a Portal API user.
  4. Click Save.
    The Employees display in the Employees List View and Users Detail View pages.

View and Contact Employees

  1. Click Employees from the Profile menu (located in the upper right corner of the ProTracker window).
    The Employees Search List displays.
  2. Use the Basic Search or Advanced Search to filter the list of Employees.
  3. Click the Employee Name to see additional information about an employee.
    The Employee Detail View displays.
  4. Click the Employee's Email Address to send him/her an email message. The Quick Compose form displays.
  5. Complete the form and click Send.

To go back to the Employees List View from the Employees Detail View click Employees on the Profile menu.

Manage Employee records

As an administrator of ProTracker, you can manage Employee records using the following instructions.

To…

Then…

Edit or update employee information
  1. On the Employees Detail View, click Edit.
    The Employees Edit View displays.
  2. Update the information as needed, and click Save.

 

Suppress an Employee record

On the Employees Detail View, click Suppress.

Export Employee records
  1. On the Employees List View, click the selection box associated with each record you want to export.
  2. Click Export and follow the process detailed in Exporting data.

Update the status of multiple employees
  1. On the Employees List View, click the selection box associated with each record you want to update.
  2. Click Mass Update and follow the process detailed in Editing and suppressing multiple records.

Duplicate a record
  1. On the Employees Detail View, click Duplicate.
    The Employees Edit View displays.
  2. Modify the information if needed.
  3. Click Save.

Duplication is a convenient way of creating a new employee. You can change the information in the duplicate record to create the new employee.
