Overview

Use the Cash Flow module to track a client’s income and expenses.

Availability

The Cash Flow module is available to users of the following editions:

  • Standard
  • Professional
  • Enterprise

Actions

Create a Cash Flow record

  1. Access the Cash Flow Create form by doing one of the following:
    • Click Goals & Objectives > Cash Flow > Create Cash Flow on the Navigation menu.
    • Click Clients > View Clients on the Navigation menu, click the related Client to view its Cash Flow sub-panel, and then click Create on the Cash Flow Actions dropdown.
  2. Complete the form by entering information for the fields described below. Fields that have an asterisk (*) are required; you must enter data in the field to save the Name.
  3. Click Save.
    The Cash Flow record is created.

Client

Click the client associated with this Cash Flow.

Cash Flow Name

Describe the client’s Cash Flow such as “2013 Budget.”

File

If a spreadsheet was used to calculate the client’s cash flow, select it here.

Description

Enter additional details about the Cash Flow.

Assigned to

By default, the record is assigned to the creator of the Cash Flow record. To change the assignment, click the user on the Users Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of users matching the character(s) you enter.

Year

Enter the year this Cash Flow was effective.

Date Prepared*

On the Date Selector click the date the Cash Flow was prepared. This is a mandatory field.

Teams*

By default, your private team is assigned as the primary team. To change the primary team assignment, click the team on the Teams Popup List View (displays upon clicking the Selection arrow) or the list of teams matching the character(s) you enter.

To assign additional teams to the Cash Flow, click add (+) and click the team as described above. For more information on assigning teams, see Assigning Users and Teams to Records.

This is a mandatory field.

Income & Expense Details section

Income per Month

Enter the gross income earned by the client.

Fixed Expenses per Month

Enter the amount of money devoted to expenses that do not change over the short term, such as loan payments.

Savings per Month

Enter the amount of money saved or invested.

Variable Expenses per Month

Enter the amount of money spent on expenses that fluctuate.

Edit a Cash Flow record

Update a Cash Flow record using the following instructions.
  1. Access the Cash Flow Edit View by doing one of the following:

    • Click Goals & Objectives > Cash Flow > View Cash Flow from the Navigation menu, then click the edit (pencil) icon associated with the record you want to edit.
    • Click Clients > View Clients on the Navigation menu, click the related Client to view its Cash Flow sub-panel, and then click Edit on the Cash Flow Actions dropdown.
  2. Make changes to the Cash Flow record, using the field descriptions in Create a Cash Flow record.
  3. Click Save.
    The changes are saved to the record.

Suppress a Cash Flow record

Use the following instructions to suppress a Cash Flow record.
  1. Click Goals & Objectives > Cash Flow > View Cash Flow from the Navigation menu.
    The Cash Flow List View displays.
  2. Click the Selection box associated with the record(s) to be suppressed.
  3. Click Suppress.
    The record(s) are removed.
